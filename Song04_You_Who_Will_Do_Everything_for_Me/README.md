# You Who Will Do Everything for Me - எனக்காக யாவையும் செய்து முடிக்கப்போகிற

This song of urgent plea and confident refuge echoes Psalm 57 — “Be merciful to me, O God, be merciful to me, for in you my soul takes refuge; in the shadow of your wings I will take refuge… I will sing and make melody. Awake, my glory! Awake, O harp and lyre!” It is a cry from the midst of lions and snares, trusting God’s mercy and wings for deliverance. In the eternal day, this refuge is perfected — no more snares, only endless praise under His wings (Revelation 7:15).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, merciful King who shelters us under Your wings,
thank You for hearing our cries in the midst of lions and snares.
Be merciful, deliver us, awaken our praise.
In the eternal day, let us sing Your glory forever.
Come quickly, our Refuge and Glory.
